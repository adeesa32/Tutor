Anderson, P. (2015). Cambridge English American Empower Elementary/A2 Workbook without Answers. Cambrige University Press.
Read the text and say if the sentences are true or false.
To:Cole
From: Kevin
Subject: Re: Invitation.
Dear Cole 

Thank you very much for your invitation to visit you in the summer. I asked my parents, and they say can give me the money for the ticket, so that's great!
 
I'm writing this on my phone in a cafè in Switzerland because we're spending a week here, skiing. It's snowing now, so it seems strange to think about summer! Tell me more about where you live. Is there a lot to do there?
 
Also, what is the weather like in summer? My sister says it's always raining in the Uk, but I think she just wants to make me angry!
 
Write back soon!
 
Kevin
Cole sent Kevin an invitation to visit his country.
Kevin has money saved to pay for his ticket.
Kevin and his family are on a skiing holiday in the UK.
Kevin wants to know about the country where Cole lives.
Kevin's sister is always nice to him.
